Wowspiced

Social Media Marketing, '25 — Growing brand presence and driving sales

My Role

Position
Social Media Manager & Content Strategist
Duration
1 months (Ongoing partnership)
Team
iagmedia.lk

Overview

Wow Spiced, a fast-growing local favorite known for its affordable burgers and ice creams, partnered with iag media to establish a strong digital identity. The goal was to showcase its delicious menu, boost local awareness, and attract new walk-in customers through social media storytelling and content marketing.

Key Results

• Created 15+ UGC-style videos that boosted engagement by 180%
• Established a recognizable content style across Social Media
• 1,000+ new followers across platforms
• Improved brand recall and walk-in traffic

Tools & Skills

Photoshop · CapCut · Videography · Premier Pro · Social Media Marketing · UGC Video Strategy · Content Design
Context

The Challenge

Wow Spiced had a loyal local audience but lacked a strong social media presence to attract new customers. The brand had no content structure, inconsistent visuals, and low visibility on Google search and Maps.

Solution

Strategic Approach

We focused on authentic, snackable content that reflected Wow Spiced’s fun and youthful vibe.

Phase 1 — Awareness & Identity
  • Produced 15+ short form UGC videos showing food reactions, plating, and behind the scenes kitchen content.
  • Created a visual identity around bold colors, appetizing textures, and local slang to connect with Gen Z audiences.
Phase 2 — Growth & Optimization
  • Introduced a monthly mix of 8 videos + 4 posts for consistent visibility and engagement.
  • Revamped Google My Business with optimized keywords, photos, and updated menus.
  • Engaged with customers through comments, interactive stories, and polls to build loyalty.
Results

Impact & Outcomes

Increased local engagement and organic reach by 180%
a recognizable, modern online identity
Boosted in-store sales and repeat visits from social campaigns
