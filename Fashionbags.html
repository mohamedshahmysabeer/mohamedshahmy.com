FashionBags

Social Media Marketing, '23 — Growing brand presence and driving sales

My Role

Position
Social Media Manager & Content Strategist
Duration
January 2023 - December 2023
Team
Solo Project with Brand Owner Collaboration

Overview

FashionBags is a Sri Lankan fashion accessories brand specializing in premium handbags and leather goods. I led the complete social media strategy, creating engaging content and managing paid campaigns that grew engagement by 240% and established strong brand presence across Instagram and Facebook.

Key Results

• 240% increase in social media engagement
• 60% increase in online sales within 3 months
• 15,000+ new followers across platforms
• 3x improvement in content reach

Tools & Skills

Content Creation • Social Media Strategy • Facebook Ads • Instagram Marketing • Video Editing • Copywriting • Analytics & Reporting • Community Management
Context

The Challenge

FashionBags had a quality product line but struggled with inconsistent social media presence and low engagement rates. They needed a comprehensive strategy to build brand awareness and drive online sales.

Solution

Strategic Approach

I developed a cohesive content strategy focusing on lifestyle imagery, customer testimonials, and behind-the-scenes content. Combined with targeted paid campaigns and consistent posting schedule.

Results

Impact & Outcomes

The comprehensive social media strategy delivered measurable results across all key metrics, establishing FashionBags as a leading fashion brand in the Sri Lankan market.
